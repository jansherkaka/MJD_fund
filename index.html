Our Process

  • 01

    Pre-Qualification

    During our first call, we’ll pre-qualify your project to understand what you need and see if we're a good match. If we are, we'll quickly put together an offering memorandum (OM) to speed up the financing process.

  • 02

    Finding the Right Match

    After the initial call, we’ll understand your requirements and match them with our preferred lenders. This ensures that both parties benefit from the deal and that we find the best fit for your project

  • 03

    Moving Forward

    Once we get terms from lenders, you decide if you want to move forward. If you do, we’ll guide your project through the necessary steps, whether it’s standard or expedited underwriting and due diligence. We're with you from start to finish to make sure everything goes smoothly.
